# Pricing

Purroxy costs $3.89/month after a 7-day free trial. Contributors use it free forever.

## The plan

There is one plan. It includes everything: unlimited sites, capabilities, executions, and devices. Zero-knowledge credential security is always on.

When you download Purroxy, you get 7 days free with full access. After that, it's $3.89/month billed through Stripe. Cancel anytime from your account.

## Contributor program

If you publish a site to the public library and it gets approved, your account is upgraded to Contributor and you never pay again. If you were already on the monthly plan, billing stops immediately.

See [Publishing Sites]({% link publishing.md %}) for details.

## FAQ

### What happens after the trial?

Your account converts to the $3.89/month plan automatically. If you cancel before the 7 days are up, you won't be charged.

### Can I cancel?

Yes, anytime. Go to the Billing Portal in your account settings. There are no contracts or commitments.

### How does the contributor program work?

Build a site in the desktop app, submit it to the public library, and wait for a review. Most reviews take a few days. Once approved, your site goes live and your account is free forever.

### What counts as an approvable site?

Any site that targets a real, publicly accessible website and provides at least one genuinely useful capability. It does not need to be perfect. If it works and helps people, it qualifies.
